# 📊Online-Sales-Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard analyzes online sales performance across categories, regions, and customer behavior in India. It helps identify profit drivers, sales trends, and regional opportunities while also highlighting critical business gaps like low profit margins.

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Track overall sales, profit, and quantity
- Identify top-performing categories & sub-categories
- Analyze regional (state-wise & city-wise) performance
- Understand customer purchasing & payment behavior
- Evaluate profitability against targets

🔍 Key Insights:
- 🖨️ Printers, Bookcases, and Sarees are the top revenue-generating sub-categories
- 👕 Clothing dominates quantity share (~62%), meaning high demand but not necessarily high profit
- 💳 COD and UPI are major payment modes, indicating customer preference for flexible payments
- ⚠️ Profit Margin is extremely low (0.01 vs 0.15 target)

→ This is a major business concern (high sales ≠ high profitability)

💡 Business Takeaway:

The company is generating strong sales volume, but profitability is critically low — pricing, discounting, or cost structure needs immediate optimization.

🔍 Key Insights:

- Q1 shows highest sales, followed by a dip in Q2 & Q3
- Recovery seen in Q4, especially in Electronics

💰 Profitability:

Some sub-categories like:
- Tables
- Accessories
- Furnishings
  are reducing overall profit (visible in waterfall drop)

🏆 Category Contribution:
- Electronics generates highest sales (~818K)
- Clothing and Furniture contribute moderately

📅 Monthly Trend:
- Peak months: January & March
- Lowest sales: July–August (seasonal dip)

💡 Business Takeaway:

High-revenue categories are not always high-profit. Certain sub-categories are dragging profitability down and need cost or pricing review.

## 📊 Overall Business Insights
- 📈 Sales are strong but profitability is critically low
- 🛒 High-selling products are not always profitable
- 📉 Certain sub-categories are profit killers
- 🌍 Regional imbalance in profit vs sales
- 💳 Payment trends show reliance on COD & UPI
- 👕 Clothing drives volume, Electronics drives revenue
- 🚀 Recommendations
- 💰 Optimize pricing for low-margin products
- ❌ Reduce losses from underperforming sub-categories
- 📦 Focus marketing on high-profit items
- 🌍 Improve operational efficiency in low-profit states
- 💳 Encourage prepaid payments to reduce COD risk
